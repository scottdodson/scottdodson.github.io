04.29.2025 - This post is a live journal of my work to create a receipt reader.

I have always been something of a data nerd. Tracking exchanges over time is a fantastic way to 'reality check' understanding and presumptions. I have been wanting to do sort of analysis for grocery purchases. Purchase data exists, I am certain, at the retail provider, but I have not (yet) seen an easy way to get detailed purchase history from most retailers. Amazon comes closest! Their purchase history functionality is great.

Last week I started doing experiments with a Jupyter Notebook and some rubber ducking with ChatGPT and Gemini. Both OpenAI and Google LLM products convert a picture of a receipt into fields and json beautifully. I am working with Gemini today to break down how to use Tesseract to replicate the LLM result.

Why bother? I am curious, and diving into the weeds of how things work is one of my things.
